TAKE A BREAK

It's important to take breaks while you're studying,especially if you're feeling tired
or frustrated. Give your brain and body a chance to refresh so you can approach the material
with energy and focus.Taking intentional breaks has been linked to better retention, increased
attention, and boosts in energy.


BENEFITS OF TAKING A BREAK

Increases productivity.
Improves mental health and well-being.
Employees feel more valued by their organization and supervisor if they promote taking breaks.
Increases job satisfaction.
Restores focus and attention, especially for long term goals.
Can prevent decision fatigue.
Increases creativity.
